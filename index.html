About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Department of Computer Science, NTNU, Norway. Prior to that, I was an ERCIM postdoctoral fellow also at the NTNU. My research interests include data mining and searching in big data, and algorithm design. I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Hunan University, China in 2017. I received my Bachelor degree and Master degree in computer science at Hanoi University of Science and Technology, Vietnam in 2004 and 2009, respectively. I was a lecturer at Hanoi University of Industry, Vietnam from 2005 to 2013.
